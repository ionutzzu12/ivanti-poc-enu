createdAt: Wed Apr 22 2026 10:16:35 GMT+0000 (Coordinated Universal Time)

Procedure

1. Log in to Microsoft Endpoint Manager [https://endpoint.microsoft.com](https://endpoint.microsoft.com/).
2. In the Microsoft Endpoint Manager admin center page, go to Home > Endpoint Security > Conditional Access.
3. Click Policies and then click + New Policy.

<Image src="Resources/Images/MSEM_01_AddnewPol.png" size="27" isUploading="false" initialPath="Resources/Images/MSEM_01_AddnewPol.png" githubPath="ENU/Resources/Images/MSEM_01_AddnewPol.png" position="flex-start" indent="2"></Image>

4. Enter the Name of the conditional access policy.

<Image src="Resources/Images/MSEM_02_AddnewPol.png" size="23" isUploading="false" initialPath="Resources/Images/MSEM_02_AddnewPol.png" githubPath="ENU/Resources/Images/MSEM_02_AddnewPol.png" position="flex-start" indent="2"></Image>

5. In Assignments, click to assign the policy to users and groups.

<Image src="Resources/Images/MSEM_03_AddnewPol.png" size="21" isUploading="false" initialPath="Resources/Images/MSEM_03_AddnewPol.png" githubPath="ENU/Resources/Images/MSEM_03_AddnewPol.png" position="flex-start" indent="2"></Image>

6. Click Cloud apps or actions and then click Select. Search for and select the apps required to be protected.

<Image src="Resources/Images/MSEM_04_AddnewPol.png" size="28" isUploading="false" initialPath="Resources/Images/MSEM_04_AddnewPol.png" githubPath="ENU/Resources/Images/MSEM_04_AddnewPol.png" position="flex-start" indent="2"></Image>

7. Click Conditions and then click Device Platform. Select the appropriate device platforms.

<Image src="Resources/Images/MSEM_05_AddnewPol.png" size="22" isUploading="false" initialPath="Resources/Images/MSEM_05_AddnewPol.png" githubPath="ENU/Resources/Images/MSEM_05_AddnewPol.png" position="flex-start" indent="2"></Image>

8. In the New conditional access policy page > Access controls, click Grant and make the access and block selections.

<Image src="Resources/Images/MSEM_06_AddnewPol.png" size="10" isUploading="false" initialPath="Resources/Images/MSEM_06_AddnewPol.png" githubPath="ENU/Resources/Images/MSEM_06_AddnewPol.png" position="flex-start" indent="2"></Image>

9. To enable the new policy, click On.

<Image src="Resources/Images/MSEM_07_AddnewPol.png" size="27" isUploading="false" initialPath="Resources/Images/MSEM_07_AddnewPol.png" githubPath="ENU/Resources/Images/MSEM_07_AddnewPol.png" position="flex-start" indent="2"></Image>

10. Click Create.
