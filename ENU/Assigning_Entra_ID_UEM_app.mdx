To complete the assign user setup:

1. Click the MobileIron UEM app you created in Step 2 in [Entra ID Windows 10 Unified Endpoint Management Setup](Entra_ID_Windows_10_Unified_Endpoint_Management_Setup.mdx)
2. Under MDM user scope, assign it to your customized user groups or select **All**.
